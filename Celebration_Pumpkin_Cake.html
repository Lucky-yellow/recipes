Celebration Pumpkin Cake

Makes 16 servings



2 packages (18.5 oz) spice, gingerbread, or carrot cake mix plus ingredients to prepare mixes

1 can (about 16 oz) solid-pack pumpkin

¼ cup butter, softened

2 containers (16 oz each) cream cheese frosting

1/3 cup caramel topping (see below)

Pecan halves



  1. Preheat over to 350F. Grease and flour 4 (9-inch) round cake pans

  2. Prepare cake mixes according to package instructions. Add pumpkin and butter to batter; beat with electric mixer at medium speed 2 minutes. Divide batter evenly among prepared pans. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until toothpick inserted into centers comes out clean. Cool 5 minutes on wire racks. Remove from pans; cool completely.

  3. Place one cake layer on serving plate; cover with frosting. Repeat layers, ending with frosting. Frost side of cake. Spread warm caramel topping over top of cake, letting some caramel drip down side. Garnish with pecan halves.





Caramel Topping

7 oz caramels

¼ cup whipping cream

Stir over low heat until smooth

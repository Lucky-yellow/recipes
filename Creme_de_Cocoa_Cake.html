Crème de Cocoa Cake



1 chocolate cake mix

16-oz jar of fudge ice cream topping

1 8-oz cool whip

1 cup crème de cocoa


Prepare according to package directions except substitute crème de cocoa for a little over half of the water (example if the recipe calls for 1 1/4 cups water, substitute crème de cocoa for ¾ of the cup of water).


The baking time will be shorter than the package directions because of the alcohol in the cake.


Let cool. Spread fudge topping over top of cake.


Add ¼ cup of crème de cocoa to the cool whip and spread on top.


Refrigerate.








Variation: Crème de Menthe Cake


Use white cake mix in place of the chocolate cake mix, and crème de menthe in place of the crème de cocoa.



Variation: Crème de Noya Cake


Use white cake mix in place of the chocolate cake mix, and crème de noya in place of the crème de cocoa.

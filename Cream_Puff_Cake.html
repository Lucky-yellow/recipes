Boil water and butter; remove from heat. Add flour and mix thoroughly. Add eggs to flour mixture, beating after each egg. Spread batter evenly in 9 X 13 pan. Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes. Cool. Mix cream cheese, instant vanilla pudding and milk together. Spread on cooled cake. Add whipped topping. Drizzle chocolate syrup on top. Refrigerate.

Coconut Macaroon Brownies


1 (19.5 oz.) pkg. brownie mix (vegetable oil, eggs and water as directed on brownie mix package)

1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese, softened

2/3 c. sugar

2 T. flour

2 eggs

2 c. flaked coconut

1 c. chopped almonds


Glaze:

1 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips

2 t. vegetable oil


Preheat oven to 350. Grease a 9 x 13‚Äù baking pan; set aside. In a large bowl, combine brownie mix, oil, eggs and water as directed on package; mix until blended. Spread batter in prepared pan. In a medium bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar, flour and eggs; beat until smooth. Stir in coconut and chopped almonds. Drop spoonfuls of cream cheese mixture over brownie batter; spread evenly. Bake 43-48 minutes or until lightly browned and center is firm to the touch. Let brownies cool in pan when preparing the glaze. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine chocolate chips and oil; cook on high for 1 minute, stirring every 20-30 seconds. When melted, drizzle over cooled brownies. Refrigerate any leftover brownies. Makes 24 brownies.

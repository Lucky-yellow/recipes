Country Carrot Cake


2 cups sugar

1 ½ cups oil

4 eggs

2 t. baking soda

2 t. cinnamon

1 t. salt

2 cups flour


2 T. rum or vanilla (I use vanilla)

3 cups chopped carrots

1 cup chopped dates (I use golden raisins)

1 cup chopped pecans


Combine sugar, oil, and eggs. Add soda, cinnamon, salt, and flour. Stir in rum, carrots, dates, and pecans. Bake 35-45 minutes (3 round pans) at 350 degrees. You will need to bake this cake until it tests done. In a 9 x 13” pan it takes about an hour. Don’t overbake.


Frosting

8 oz. cream cheese

½ cup butter

3 – 3 ½ cups powdered sugar.

2 T. rum or vanilla

1 cup pecans ( I leave out)

Apples Bars


2 1\2 c. flour

1 tbsp. sugar

1 tsp. salt

1 c. shortening


  • Mix above ingredients like pie crust.

  • Put 1 egg yolk (save white) in 2/3 measuring cup and add milk to fill.

  • Add to crust mixture.

  • Mix well.

  • Roll out 1/2 of dough to fit cookie sheet.

  • Add 2 handfuls of wheaties or corn flakes on top of rolled out crust.

  • Top crust with approx. 7 large sliced apples.

  • Mix 1 c. sugar and 1 tsp. cinnamon and sprinkle on apples.

  • Roll out remaining crust for top.

  • Beat egg white until stiff and spread on top of crust.

  • Bake @ 350 for 40 min.

  • Mix 1 c. powdered sugar and water and spread on top of warm crust

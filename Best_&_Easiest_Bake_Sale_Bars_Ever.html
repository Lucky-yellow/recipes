Best & EASIEST Bake Sale Bars Ever


These only take 35 minutes tops from start to finish and they are GREAT!

Preheat oven to 350, grease a 9 x 13 pyrex pan with non-stick spray

Mix:
1 box of yellow cake mix
1 softened stick of butter or margarine with at least 65% vegetable oil content
1 cup chocolate chips
2 eggs

Press dough into pan and bake 30-35 minutes and WA-LA! Cool before cutting!

You can make these with different cake/chip flavor combinations too!

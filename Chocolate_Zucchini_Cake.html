Chocolate Zucchini Cake

½ Cup Butter 4 Tablespoons Cocoa

½ Cup Cooking Oil ½ Tsp Baking Powder

1 ¾ Cup Sugar 2 Tsp Soda

2 Eggs beaten ½ Tsp Cinnamon

2 Tsp Vanilla 2 Cups Zucchini shredded

½ Cup Sour milk ½ Cup Chocolate Chips

2 ½ Cup Flour

Cream butter, oil and sugar in large mixing bowl. Add beaten eggs, vanilla and sour milk. Add dry ingredients to egg mixture mix well. Stir in zucchini and choc chips. I add chopped walnuts to the bottom of the Bundt pan or on the top of the cake before baking.

Bake in greased pan (I use either Bundt pan or 9x13) 350 degrees for about 45 minutes.

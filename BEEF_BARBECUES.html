BEEF BARBECUES

1 CAN OF TOMATO SOUP

1 ONION, DICED

1 T. WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE

1 ½ T. LEMON JUICE

1 ½ T. VINEGAR

½ C. BROWN SUGAR

½ C. CATSUP

¼ TSP CHILI POWDER (ORIGINAL RECIPE CALLED FOR ½ TSP)

STACK OF CELERY

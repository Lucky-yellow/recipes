Chocolate Ice Cream Cake

  1. Package Brownie Mix

Â½ Gallon Ice Cream, your favorite, I used Oreo Cookie Dough

  1. 16oz Frosting


Bake brownie mix according to recipe however a few minutes short so the brownies are soft.

Do not overcook them. Cool for 10 minutes.

Line a large bowl with saran wrap.

Take ice cream out and let soften while doing the next step (about 10 minutes).

Lightly press 2/3rd of the brownie mix around the bowl to form a bowl.

Empty the ice cream into the brownie bowl and firmly press to fill.

Press the remaining brownie on the bottom on the top of the ice cream.

Set entire cake in the freezer for about 2 hours.

Take out and frost.

Ready to eat!

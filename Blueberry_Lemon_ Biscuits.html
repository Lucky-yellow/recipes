Lemon Blueberry Biscuits


2 cups flour

⅓ cup sugar

2 teaspoons baking powder

½ teaspoon baking soda

¼ teaspoon salt

1 carton (8 ounces) lemon yogurt

1 egg, lightly beaten

¼ cup butter or margarine, melted

1 teaspoon grated lemon peel

1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

GLAZE:

½ cup confectioners’ sugar

1 tablespoon lemon juice

½ teaspoon grated lemon peel


In a large bowl, combine dry ingredients. Combine yogurt, egg, butter and lemon peel; stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Fold in blueberries. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto a greased baking sheet. Bake at 400° for 15-18 minutes or until lightly browned. Combine glaze ingredients; drizzle over warm biscuits. Yield: 1 dozen. Enjoy!

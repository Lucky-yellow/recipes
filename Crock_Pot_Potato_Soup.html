Crock-Pot Potato Soup



2 packages

“Simply Potatoes”, Cubed Potatoes with Onion

½ pound

Crisply cooked bacon

8 oz.

Light cream cheese, softened

3 cans

Sodium reduced chicken broth (14.5 oz. per can)

1 can

Sodium reduced cream of chicken soup (10.75 oz. can)

¼ tsp.

Pepper



Place the potatoes with onion and the bacon in crock-pot.

In a large mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, broth, cream of chicken soup and pepper.

Add to crock-pot and stir together with potato mixture.

Cook on low for 8-10 hours or high for 4-5 hours.

Mash potatoes slightly before serving to thicken the soup.

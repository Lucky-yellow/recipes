Curried Sweet Potato and Lentil Stew


3 c. 1-inch cubes of peeled raw sweet potatoes

1 1/2 c. baby cut carrots

1 small onion, finely chopped (1/4 c.)

3/4 c. dried lentils, sorted and rinsed

2 tsp. olive oil

1 T. curry powder

1 tsp. ground cumin

1/2 tsp. salt

1/4 tsp. pepper

1 tsp. finely chopped gingerroot

1 clove garlic, finely chopped

1 can (14 1/2 oz.) chicken broth

1 package (10 oz.) frozen green beans, thawed

1/2 c. vanilla or plain yogurt


Mix sweet potatoes, carrots, onion, and lentils in 3 1/2 to 6-qt. slow cooker.


Heat oil in 8-in. skillet over medium heat. Add curry powder, cumin, salt, pepper, gingerroot and garlic. Cook 1 minute, stirring constantly. Stir in broth. Pour mixture into slow cooker; stir.


Cover and cook on low setting for 5 to 6 hours or until vegetables and lentils are tender.


Turn heat to HIGH ; add green beans. Cover and cook about 15 minutes or until green beans are crisp-tender. Serve topped with yogurt.

Baked Stuffed Pumpkin


1 pkg Stove Top Stuffing mix

1 ½ cups water

¼ cup butter

½ cup wild rice

1 ½ cups water

½ cup chopped onion

½ pound hot Italian sausage, browned

¼ cup dried cranberries

¼ cup chopped apples

1 medium pie pumpkin

¼ tsp salt

¼ tsp garlic powder


In a medium saucepan, prepare stuffing mix according to package directions with first 1 ½ cups water and butter.


In a large saucepan, bring wild rice, second 1 ½ cups water and onion to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for about 30-45 minutes or until tender.


While that is simmering, remove sausage from casing, crumble and brown in a large frying pan. Drain the sausage. Combine stuffing mix, wild rice, sausage and dried cranberries.


Wash the pumpkin and cut a circle around the stem. Remove the top and set aside. Remove the fibers and seeds. Prick inside the pumpkin with a fork; sprinkle with salt and garlic powder. Fill the pumpkin with the stuffing mixture and replace the lid.


Place stuffed pumpkin in a 9 X 13-inch baking dish; add ½ cup of water to the bottom of the pan. Bake at 350 for 1 ½ to 2 hours, or until tender. Add more water to bottom of pan, if necessary, to prevent sticking.


Serve pumpkin on a plate decorated with fallen, colored leaves. Scoop out pumpkin meat along with the hot stuffing mixture. Pumpkin retains its heat for a long time, so this is great for fall potlucks or bonfires!

Cole Slaw Salad


½ cup oil (I used Olive Oil)

½ cup sugar (I used ¼ cup Splenda)

½ cup vinegar

Season packet from Ramen Noodles

Put in bottle and shake I made this ahead and put in refrigerate to get cold


  1. Bag Cole Slaw Mix

½ cup Sun Flower Seeds

½ cup Toasted Sliced Almonds

1 Bunch of green onions diced

Ramen Noodles Crushed


Mix all together about ½ hour before eating.

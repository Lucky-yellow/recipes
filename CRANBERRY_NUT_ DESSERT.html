In a bowl, combine the flour sugar and salt. Add cranberries and nuts; toss to coat. Stir in the butter, eggs and extract (mixture will e very thick if using frozen berries). Spread into a greased 9-inch pie plate. Bake at 350 for 40 minutes or until a wooden pick inserted near the center comes out clean. Serve warm with whipped cream or ice cream if desired. Yield: 8 servings



RANBERRY NUT DESSERT


1 c. all-purpose flour

1 c. sugar

¼ tsp. Salt

2 c. fresh or frozen cranberries

½ c. chopped walnuts

½ c. butter or margarine, melted

2 eggs, beaten

½ tsp. almond extract

Whipped cream or ice cream, optional

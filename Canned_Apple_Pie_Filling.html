Canned Apple Pie Filling


Makes 6 quarts


4 ½ cups sugar

1 cup cornstarch

2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg

1 teaspoon salt

10 cups water

3 tablespoons lemon juice

2-3 drops yellow food coloring

51/2 – 6 pounds tart apples, peeled, cored, and sliced


  1. In large saucepan, mix sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt until well blended.


  1. Stir in water. Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened and bubbly. Add lemon juice and food coloring.


  1. Pack apples into hot quart jars, leaving 1” headspace.


  1. Fill with hot syrup, leaving ½” headspace. (Use spatula to help distribute syrup) Adjust lids.


  1. Process in boiling water bath 20 minutes.


  1. Here are the directions for baking to attach to jars: Prepare pastry for a 2 crust 9” pie. Line pie plate with pastry, add 1 quart apple pie filling. Adjust top crust cutting slits for steam to escape. Seal. Bake in preheated 400 degree oven for 50 minutes.

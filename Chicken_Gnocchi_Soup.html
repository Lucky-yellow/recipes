Chicken Gnocchi Soup (Jennifer’s Version) To serve approximately 8-12 bowls

1 whole chicken—baked and meat taken off bones

½ cup chopped onions

2 cloves chopped garlic

½ cup chopped celery

½ cup chopped or shredded carrots

½ cup butter

6 tablespoons flour

1 quart chicken broth

1 pint half and half

1 pint milk (2% or whole)

1 pkg. gnocchi (cut in half to stretch) approximately 3-4 cups

2 chicken bouillon cubes

Parsley

1 Tablespoon garlic powder or salt

Salt/pepper to taste


Melt butter in large stockpot. Cook vegetables until tender. Add flour and stir and cook for approximately 1 minute. Add in chicken broth and whisk until smooth. Add bouillon cubes and parsley. Add chicken and gnocchi and cook for approximately 10 minutes. Add in cream and milk. Heat through and simmer until flavors are incorporated. Add additional skim milk or chicken broth to desired consistency.

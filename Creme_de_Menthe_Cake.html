Crème de Menthe Cake



1 white cake mix

16-oz jar of fudge ice cream topping

1 8-oz cool whip

1 cup crème de menthe


Prepare according to package directions except substitute crème de menthe for a little over half of the water (example if the recipe calls for 1 1/4 cups water, substitute crème de menthe for ¾ of the cup of water).


The baking time will be shorter than the package directions because of the alcohol in the cake.


Let cool. Spread fudge topping over top of cake.


Add ¼ cup of crème de menthe to the cool whip and spread on top.


Refrigerate.

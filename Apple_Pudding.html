Apple Pudding


6 c. apples, pared and sliced

½ c. water

2 t. lemon juice

¾ c. all-purpose flour

1 c. sugar

1 t. cinnamon

½ c. butter


Place prepared apples in a buttered 9-inch baking dish or 2-quart casserole. Pour water and lemon juice over apples. Place flour, sugar, and cinnamon in a small bowl and cut butter in with a fork or pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle over apples. Bake in a 375 degree oven 40 to 50 minutes or until apples bubble and top is golden brown. Serve warm with cream or vanilla ice cream.


Yield: 6 servings
